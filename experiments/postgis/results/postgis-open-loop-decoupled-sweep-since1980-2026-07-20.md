## Claim boundary

Open-loop Poisson arrivals generated by pgbench against the same durable mixed spatial workload from a separate container. PostgreSQL/WAL deltas and per-container resources are sampled. Passing means no database transaction failure, a skipped-arrival rate within the declared admission budget, and observed completion p99 at or below the stated latency limit on the reported host. It is not a universal SLA.
